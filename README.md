# IRIS: AI for Primary Care

**Smarter care. Earlier action. Better outcomes.**

IRIS is a modular, AI-powered risk prediction system built for the messy, multimorbid reality of primary care. We combine structured EHR data with patient-reported outcomes (PROs) to surface actionable insights ‚Äî not just predictions.

- **Built for Frontline Care**  
  Unlike traditional risk models designed for population health analysts, IRIS is optimized for in-clinic use ‚Äî real-time, interpretable, and embedded into clinical workflows.

- **Multimorbidity-Aware Modeling**  
  We go beyond single-disease prediction to capture complex, compounding risks in aging and underserved populations.

- **Explainable by Design**  
  Transparent SHAP-backed explainability, fairness metrics, and metadata layers help support clinical trust and regulatory readiness.

IRIS is in active development using a dual-source strategy: model training leverages large-scale EHR data from over 20 million patients, while clinical workflows and patient-reported outcome integration is co-developed with a network of independent primary care clinics. This approach allows us to model complexity at population scale, while ensuring usability, transparency, and trust are informed by the realities of frontline care.
